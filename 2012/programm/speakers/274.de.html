QGIS auf Android

Marco Bernasocchi

Marco Bernasocchi started contributing to QGIS in 2010, during his last graduated student year at the University of Zurich, when he developed multiple plugins for his Master thesis and started porting QGIS to android as part of a Google Summer of Code project. After getting a MSc degree in Geography with specialisation in GIS he continued the port of QGIS to android and is now the main developer behind the project. Marco works as a freelancer under www.opengis.ch
