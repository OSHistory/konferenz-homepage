Interoperability for web portrayal services : a use case with deegree3

Concisely, interoperability is the ability of diverse systems to work together. But how to do that when the work is about cartographic styling? This workshop wants to emonstrate how portrayal interoperability is possible within a modern spatial data infrastructure through OpenGIS standards from the Open Geospatial Consortium like OGC Styled Layer Descriptor WMS profile and Symbology Encoding (OGC SLD/SE 1.1). The workshop will guide attendees all along a fictional use case to discover how to inter-operate OGC SLD compliant systems and what are the cartograpĥic abilities of OGC SE. To conclude, an overview of what may be the evolution of these abilities will be experienced.
